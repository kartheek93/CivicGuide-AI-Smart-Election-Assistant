## Election Commission of India (ECI)
The ECI is an autonomous authority enacted under the Constitution (Article 324) responsible for administering Union and State elections. It ensures free and fair elections. State Election Commissions administer local body elections (Panchayats and Municipalities).

## Voting System & Technology
- **EVMs (Electronic Voting Machines):** Used since 1997. Saves time and prevents booth capturing.
- **VVPAT (Voter-verified paper audit trail):** Added to ensure voters can cross-check their cast vote.
- **NOTA (None of the Above):** Introduced in 2013, allows citizens to cast a negative vote against all candidates.
- **Indelible Ink:** Applied to the left index finger to prevent double-voting. Contains photo-sensitive silver nitrate.
